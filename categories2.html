Course Overview

This course introduces students to the principles, methodologies, tools, and best practices of project management. Students will learn how to initiate, plan, execute, monitor, and close projects effectively and efficiently. Practical exercises and real-world case studies will be used to reinforce understanding and application of project management concepts.

What you'll learn

Scope & Quality Management - beginer to advanced

Time & Schedule Management - beginer to advanced

Cost & Resource Management - beginer to advanced
